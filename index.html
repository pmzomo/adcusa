Who We Are

The Voice of the African Diaspora in America

Founded in 2015 in Durham, North Carolina, the African Diaspora Coalition of USA (ADCUSA) was created to harness the vast potential of the African diaspora community in driving meaningful change — both in America and across the African continent.

Led by Co-Founder and Executive Director Dr. Philip Mwalali MD, ADCUSA brings together African professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders to build networks, cultural centers, and economic opportunities that transcend borders.
