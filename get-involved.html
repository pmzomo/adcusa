Donate to ADCUSA

As a 100% volunteer-driven 501(c)(3) public charity, every dollar you contribute goes directly into programs that serve the African diaspora community. Your donation is fully tax-deductible.

Your Gift at Work

$25 Supports youth leadership workshop materials for 5 students
$50 Helps host a professional networking event for diaspora members
$100 Funds a cultural center program event for the community
$500+ Sponsors a full forum or community development initiative

ADCUSA is a recognized IRS 501(c)(3) public charity. EIN: 46-4204919. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. We are a 100% volunteer organization — zero overhead on staff salaries.
